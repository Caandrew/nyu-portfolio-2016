WORK

SNAP ARt

Context

Snapchat filters are digital overlays that can be applied to photos and videos — taken in the app. When taking selfies, the app uses facial recognition to map point clouds to your face, allowing you to apply things like animal features and accessories to your likeness.

Challenge

Show people that by turning their camers away from themselves, and pointing it at the world, the spaces they occupy can become works of art.

Experience

Images of the world overlayed with filters; digital works of art rooted on space and place.
