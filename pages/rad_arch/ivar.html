WORK

IKEA HQ

Visualizing time — in real time

CONTEXT //

The IVAR chair is one of IKEA's most iconic furniture offerings; made from pieces of knotted wood, it also serves as a reminder of the furniture giant's humble beginnings

CHALLENGE //

Create an installation for the IKEA headquartes' amphitheatre that incorporates the ivar chair - and activates the space.

EXPERIENCE //

Countries with IKEA locations are each represented by a clock, which displays their local time. Minute by minute, hour by hour incoming sunlight filters through color-tinted glass, bathing the room in an ever-changing, time-series, visualization.
