WORK

EMOTION AT MOMA

CONTEXT //

We are bombarded constantly with signs, symbols, and design decisions that dictate how we use and emotionally connect to the spaces that we move through every day.

CHALLENGE //

Use physical means to reveal hidden information and influence a given space.

EXPERIENCE //

Installed [guerilla style] in the sculpture garden at the Museum of Modern Art in New York City. The artist asks that people interact with her work by contributing their own emotional data to this low-tech visualization.
