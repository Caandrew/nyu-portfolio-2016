WORK

BESPOKE NOTEBOOKS

Context //

A social experiment that takes place in various public spaces.

Challenge //

Find a way to engage strangers in meaningful conversation.

Experience //

Notebooks are hand bound in public places to illicit curiosity in others, prompting them to approach and inquire about this action.Once engaged, they are asked to describe themselves in a two part statement, which is then reworded and used to label the [block printed] pie chart on the cover. Subsequently they are given the bespoke notebook, a relic of their conversation with the conversation. * The cover art is pre-printed and the notebook is compiled ahead of time
