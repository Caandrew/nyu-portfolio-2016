WORK

ENVISIONING HOLOGRAMS; TETRIS TRACKER

CONTEXT //

With Delta’s mobile app, you can track checked bags, moment to moment, in real time.

CHALLENGE //

Baggage claim is the worst; everybody knows it. How might we change the way people perceive this unique location?

EXPERIENCE //

This installation creates a functional, and fun, baggage claim experience. When a bag drops, a new tetromino is added to the players queue. When that player’s luggage hits the carousel their game is over, and they’re given their final score and Tetris Tracker ranking.
